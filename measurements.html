Find Your Perfect Fit – The Coin Method

Use a simple coin to measure your nail width for a flawless custom press‑on fit.

How to Measure

Step 1

Place a US quarter (or similar coin) next to your thumbnail.

Step 2

Measure the distance from the edge of the coin to the tip of your nail.

Step 3

Note the measurement and use it when ordering your custom kit.
