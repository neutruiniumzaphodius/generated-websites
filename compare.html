Medigap vs. Advantage

Freedom of Choice

Medigap plans give you the freedom to see any doctor that accepts Medicare. No network restrictions mean you can get care wherever you prefer.

Predictable Budgeting.

With Medigap, you pay a fixed monthly premium and know exactly what your out‑of‑pocket costs will be for services covered by Medicare.

Additional Benefits

Medicare Advantage plans often include extra benefits such as vision, dental, hearing, and prescription drug coverage, all in one plan.
