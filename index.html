Why Scorpions Matter

From desert dunes to rainforest canopies

Evolutionary Marvels

Scorpions have survived 400 million years, making them living fossils of the arthropod world.

Ecological Role

As nocturnal predators, they control insect populations and maintain ecosystem balance.

Amazon Adaptations

Unique humidity‑tolerant species thrive in the rainforest floor, showcasing remarkable versatility.

Venom Science

Their venom contains peptides with promising medical applications, from pain relief to cancer research.

Conservation Status

Habitat loss threatens many species; awareness and protection are essential for their future.
