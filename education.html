Part A – Hospital Insurance

Part A covers inpatient hospital stays, skilled‑nursing facility care, hospice, and some home health services. In 2026 the deductible remains $1,600, and there are no monthly premiums for most beneficiaries who have paid Medicare taxes.

Download Part A Guide (PDF)

Part B – Medical Insurance

Part B helps cover doctors’ services, outpatient care, preventive services, and medical supplies. The standard monthly premium for 2026 is $164.90. Enroll online, by phone, or at your local Social Security office.

Download Part B Guide (PDF)

Part D – Prescription Drug Coverage

Part D provides prescription drug coverage through private plans. In 2026 the standard benefit includes a $5,000 deductible and a $5,000 coverage gap limit. Compare plans to find the best fit for your medication needs.

Download Part D Guide (PDF)
