Patty Alvarez

Trusted Medicare Advisor in Nevada

My Journey

Born and raised in Las Vegas, I’ve spent the last two decades helping seniors navigate the complexities of Medicare. My passion began when I assisted my own grandparents in finding the right plan, turning a confusing process into a family celebration of health security.

After earning a degree in Health Administration, I joined a leading insurance brokerage where I specialized in Medicare Advantage and Medigap solutions. In 2015 I founded Alvarez Medicare Solutions to bring personalized, compassionate guidance to Nevada’s senior community.

My Personal Mission

My goal is simple: empower every senior I work with to make confident, informed Medicare choices that protect their health and their wallet. I believe that clear, compassionate guidance turns what can be a daunting process into a seamless experience.
