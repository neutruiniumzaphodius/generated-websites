Our Story

Founders

Marian and Luca grew up together in a small town where their grandparents ran a beloved neighborhood bakery. Their love for the scent of fresh dough and the warmth of community gatherings inspired them to bring that feeling to Brookfield.

Timeline

2010

Marian starts baking with her grandmother.

2014

Luca opens his first coffee cart.

2018

They meet at a local food festival.

2020

Concept for Sweet Dough Bakery is born.

2022

First Sweet Dough location opens its doors.
