Our Mission

At Seed to Skin we believe that true beauty starts with responsibly sourced ingredients, sustainable practices, and a transparent story. Our mission is to empower women to feel confident in their skin while protecting the planet that nurtures us.

Meet Maya Collins

Maya grew up watching her grandmother hand‑craft herbal balms in a small coastal town. After studying biochemistry and traveling the world’s most biodiverse regions, she returned to California with a vision: to combine scientific rigor with nature’s wisdom. Maya personally oversees every step of the sourcing process, ensuring that each seed, herb, and oil meets the highest purity standards.
