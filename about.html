Our Story

Sunflower Organic Skincare was born from a love of nature, science, and the belief that true beauty thrives when it’s nurtured responsibly. Our journey began in a modest garden where we experimented with botanical extracts, and today we share those discoveries with the world.

Every product is a tribute to the sun‑filled fields that inspire us, crafted with integrity, transparency, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Founder & Visionary – Maya Sun

Maya grew up amidst sunflower fields in California’s Central Valley, where she learned the healing power of plants from her grandmother. After earning a degree in Botanical Sciences, she combined her passion for sustainability with cutting‑edge skincare research.

Today, Maya leads a team of ethical innovators, ensuring every bottle reflects her commitment to purity, transparency, and the planet.

“Our skin is a reflection of the world we live in. By choosing conscious ingredients, we nurture both ourselves and the earth.”
