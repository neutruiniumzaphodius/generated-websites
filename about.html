Our Missouri Roots

Founded on authenticity, grown in the heart of Kansas City.

Founder Story

Alexandra Perkins grew up watching her parents run a small boutique in downtown Kansas City. Inspired by the power of scent to evoke memory, she set out to create a curated space where collectors could find only the most authentic designer fragrances.

After studying perfumery in Paris and building relationships with legacy houses, Alexandra returned home, establishing PerforU’s regional warehouse in the heart of Missouri. Today, every bottle is sourced, verified, and packaged with the same meticulous care she learned from her family’s legacy.

Milestones

Launch – 2020

PerforU opened its first storefront and online boutique, offering a curated selection of authentic designer perfumes.

Partnership – 2021

Secured exclusive distribution agreements with Chanel, Tom Ford, and Jo Malone.

Certification – 2022

Achieved ISO 9001 certification for quality management and authenticity verification.

Expansion – 2023

Opened a state‑of‑the‑art regional warehouse in Kansas City, MO, enhancing nationwide shipping.

Community – 2024

Launched monthly fragrance workshops and local art collaborations across Missouri.
