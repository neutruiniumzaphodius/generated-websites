Our Story – From Soil to Skin

Crafting clean beauty with love, science, and sustainability.

Brand History

2018 – The Seed

Founded in a modest kitchen, Amanda began experimenting with locally‑sourced botanicals, turning a simple passion into a purpose‑driven brand.

2020 – The Growth

Our first product, Lavender Calm Serum, launched to rave reviews, proving that gentle, effective skincare can be both ethical and affordable.

2024 – The Harvest

Today we offer a full line of organic, hand‑crafted formulas, cultivated from farms across the globe and delivered in 100 % recyclable packaging.

Philosophy

We believe beauty should be kind—to your skin, to the planet, and to the people who make it possible. Our formulas are rooted in transparency, cruelty‑free practices, and a commitment to lasting sustainability.

Ingredient Sourcing

Every ingredient is carefully chosen from certified organic farms that practice regenerative agriculture.

Lavender fields in Provence, harvested by hand at peak bloom.

California almond orchards cultivated without synthetic pesticides.

Moroccan rosehip bushes thriving on biodiverse, dry‑land farms.

Meet the Team

Our small, passionate crew works together to ensure every product meets the highest standards of purity and care.
