Our Story

From the heart of Missouri to the world’s most coveted perfume houses.

Origin Story

PerforU began in 2015 as a modest pop‑up shop on the River Market in St. Louis. Two friends, Alex Monroe and Riley Hart, shared a passion for rare, artisanal fragrances and a deep love for their Missouri roots.

Guided by the rolling hills of the Ozarks and the timeless flow of the Mississippi, they set out to create a curated space where collectors could discover authentic luxury scents, all while honoring the craftsmanship of perfumers.

Founders

Alex Monroe

Riley Hart

Sustainability Commitment

PerforU pledges to minimize its environmental footprint while delivering unparalleled luxury. Our initiatives include:

  • Using 100 % recycled, gold‑stamped packaging.
  • Partnering with farms that practice regenerative agriculture.
  • Carbon‑offsetting every shipment from Missouri to the world.
  • Supporting local Missouri artisans through collaborative limited‑edition releases.

Authenticity Verification

Every PerforU fragrance undergoes a rigorous three‑step verification to guarantee provenance:

1. Source Audit

We verify each supplier’s credentials and trace the raw materials back to their origin.

2. Lab Analysis

Our in‑house chemists run GC‑MS tests to confirm composition matches the original formula.

3. Certificate of Authenticity

Every bottle ships with a blockchain‑backed certificate, ensuring immutable proof of origin.
