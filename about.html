Our Story

Luxe Interiors was founded on the belief that elegance and comfort belong together. From our modest beginnings in a small Atlanta studio, we have grown into a celebrated name in luxury interior design, celebrated for our meticulous attention to detail and commitment to timeless style. Our designs blend classic sophistication with modern sensibilities, creating spaces that feel both inviting and aspirational. Every project we undertake is guided by a deep respect for craftsmanship, high‑quality materials, and an unwavering dedication to client satisfaction. This philosophy has allowed us to transform homes, boutique hotels, and commercial spaces into enduring works of art that reflect each client’s unique vision.

Founder & Creative Director

Alexandra Blake

Alexandra Blake, the visionary behind Luxe Interiors, combines a deep-rooted passion for timeless design with a modern, sustainable approach. With over a decade of experience, she has led transformative projects ranging from boutique hotels to private residences, always emphasizing craftsmanship and client collaboration. Alexandra’s keen eye for detail and dedication to creating spaces that reflect personal narratives have earned her a reputation as a trusted authority in luxury interior design.
