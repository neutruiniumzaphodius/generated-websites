GreenSprout Organic Farm Market

From the first seed planted to the bustling market today, our journey is rooted in sustainability, community, and pure love for the land.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a family of passionate growers, GreenSprout began as a modest 5‑acre farm in Sonoma County. Guided by the belief that food should be wholesome, honest, and sustainably produced, we expanded our fields, embraced regenerative farming practices, and opened our market to share the bounty with our community.

Every leaf, fruit, and jar of honey carries the dedication of our team and the richness of our terroir. Transparency, stewardship, and connection are at the heart of everything we do.

Our Values

Sustainability

Regenerative practices that nurture soil health, water conservation, and biodiversity.

Community

Quality

Transparency

Open farms, honest labeling, and clear communication with our customers.
