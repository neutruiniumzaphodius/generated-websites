Our Story

From a modest kitchen in 1995 to a celebrated New York destination, discover the heritage that shapes every plate.

Our Journey

1995

Alessandro opens a tiny trattoria in Naples, Italy.

2002

Family emigrates to New York, launching the first Artisan’s Table.

2008

First location receives a Michelin star.

2015

Renovated space blends rustic stone with modern design.

2023

Celebrating 30 years of culinary excellence.

A Vision Rooted in Heritage

“Cooking is storytelling. Every dish I create carries the memory of my grandmother’s kitchen, the sun‑kissed olive groves of Calabria, and the bustling markets of New York. My dream has always been to share that narrative with every guest, inviting them to sit, savor, and become part of our family.”

Our Commitment to Pure Ingredients

At Artisan’s Table every ingredient is chosen with reverence. We partner with family‑run farms in Tuscany, Kalamata, and the Hudson Valley, selecting heirloom tomatoes, hand‑pressed olive oil, and organically grown herbs. Our philosophy is simple: let the land speak through flavor, and let every plate tell a story of authenticity and care.
