Safety & Compliance

NeuroVanta’s commitment to patient safety, data integrity, and regulatory excellence is built on a rigorously engineered quality system and a transparent risk‑based approach.

Quality & Risk Philosophy

Our Quality Management System (QMS) follows ISO 13485 and FDA 21 CFR 820 requirements. All processes are defined, documented, and continuously improved through internal audits and external assessments.

Risk management is performed per IEC 60601‑1‑2, using a systematic Hazard Identification, Risk Evaluation, and Mitigation workflow that is revisited at each product lifecycle stage.

Verification & Validation

Every hardware component undergoes design‑verification (DV) testing for electrical safety, biocompatibility, and reliability. Software modules are validated against predefined acceptance criteria and documented in traceable test reports.

System‑level validation includes chronic‑implant studies that demonstrate stable signal fidelity (SNR > 15 dB) and stimulation safety over 12 months of continuous use.

Security Posture

Data are encrypted at source using AES‑256 and transmitted over TLS 1.3. The closed‑loop controller enforces safety envelopes defined in the device master file, preventing out‑of‑range stimulation.

Regular penetration testing, firmware signing, and secure boot processes protect against unauthorized code execution.

Privacy Statement

All participant data are anonymized before storage. Access to raw data is restricted to authorized personnel through role‑based permissions in isolated cloud workspaces.

We comply with GDPR, HIPAA, and the latest ICH‑GCP privacy guidelines, providing audit logs for every data access event.

Clinical Ethics

Our ethics framework follows the Declaration of Helsinki and the Belmont Report. An independent Institutional Review Board (IRB) reviews every study protocol, and a Data Safety Monitoring Board (DSMB) oversees patient safety throughout the trial.

Adverse event reporting is performed in real time, with predefined escalation pathways to ensure rapid response.

Regulatory Posture

NeuroVanta maintains an FDA‑registered establishment (Q‑001) and a CE‑marked device class II (MDR 2017/745). All submissions are supported by a complete Device Master File, risk analysis (ISO 14971), and validation documentation.

We provide regulators with traceability matrices, design history files, and post‑market surveillance plans to satisfy both FDA and EMA expectations.
