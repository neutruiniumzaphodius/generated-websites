Neural Interface

High‑Fidelity ASIC

Custom 65 nm neural ASIC provides 32 channels of low‑noise amplification (≤2 µV RMS) and on‑chip digitisation.

Dynamic range up to 120 dB enables chronic recordings with <10 µV noise floor.

Stimulation Module

Adaptive Closed‑Loop Controller

Updates pulse parameters every 10 ms based on neural biomarkers, all within pre‑defined safety envelopes.

Supports up to 500 µA amplitude, 500 µs pulse width, with real‑time impedance monitoring.

BLE‑5.0 compliant module streams data at 1 kHz with AES‑128 encryption, powered by a rechargeable Li‑ion cell lasting 48 h.
