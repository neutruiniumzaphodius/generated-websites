Medigap

Predictable costs, nationwide coverage.

Predictability and Nationwide Coverage

Medigap policies fill the gaps left by Original Medicare, offering a single, predictable monthly premium that covers most out‑of‑pocket expenses, no matter where you travel in the United States.

What’s Included

Hospital stays
Skilled nursing care
Doctor’s fees
Emergency services
Prescription drugs (when paired)

What’s Not Covered

Dental care
Vision services
Hearing aids
Long‑term care
Cosmetic procedures
