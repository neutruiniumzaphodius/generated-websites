Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need both collision and comprehensive?

▼

Collision covers crash damage, while comprehensive protects against non‑collision events. Many drivers choose both for full protection.

Can I adjust my deductible?

▼

Yes, higher deductibles lower your premium. We recommend a deductible that fits your budget.

Is ride‑share coverage mandatory?

▼

Most ride‑share platforms require drivers to carry additional liability coverage. Our Ride‑Share add‑on fulfills that requirement.
