Original Medicare

Parts A & B – Your health coverage basics

2025

Part A – Hospital Insurance

Premium: $0 for most beneficiaries. Covers inpatient hospital stays, skilled nursing facility care, hospice, and some home health.

2025

Part B – Medical Insurance

Standard monthly premium: $164.90 (2025). Covers doctors' services, outpatient care, preventive services, and medical supplies.

2025

Combined A & B Costs

Total average monthly cost: $164.90 (Part B) + $0 (Part A) = $164.90. Enrollment is automatic for most people at age 65.

Frequently Asked Questions

While Part A is often premium‑free, you may choose to enroll in Part B to get coverage for doctor visits, outpatient care, and preventive services. Most beneficiaries have both.

In 2025, the Part A deductible is $1,600 per benefit period for each benefit period.

The standard Part B premium for 2025 is $164.90. Higher‑income beneficiaries may pay more based on their Modified Adjusted Gross Income.

You can enroll during your Initial Enrollment Period, the General Enrollment Period (Jan‑Mar), or during a Special Enrollment Period if you qualify.
